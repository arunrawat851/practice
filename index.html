About Creativity

Founded in 1999 and based in New Delhi, Creativity Art Gallery is a trusted platform for contemporary Indian art. Representing both emerging talent and established masters, the gallery curates works of lasting cultural and aesthetic value for collectors, curators, and art connoisseurs worldwide.

About Media Presence

For over 25 years, Creativity Art Gallery has been recognized for its curatorial work – from solo and group shows to major art fairs. Our exhibitions have sparked conversations and engaged the wider art community.
